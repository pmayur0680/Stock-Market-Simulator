What is paper trading?

A paper trade is a simulated trade that allows an investor to practice buying and selling without risking real money. Paper trading offers opportunity to practice trading and develop a profitable strategy.

Why use a stock market simulator?

Investors use stock market simulators to buy and sell stocks without using real money and sharpen trading skills. Stock market simulators allow you to start learning the market without losing anything.
